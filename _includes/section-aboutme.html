Leonardo-Unige CyberSecurity Scholarship Program

The Leonardo-Unige Cybersecurity Scholarship Program is a training program open to all students of the University of Genoa. It offers an innovative, hands-on training path that provides students with practical skills needed defend modern ICT infrastructures from cyber attacks.

The program is developed in the context of ZenHackAdemy, a collaborative project that aims to fill the gaps between traditional teaching in Cybersecurity (focused on transfering knowledge) with engaging, game-based techniques (focused on providing skills and competencies).

The Leonardo-Unige Cybersecurity Scholarship Program in strucured in two phases:

  1. Hands-on training on selected topics in Cybersecurity
    • 7 hands-on training sessions on selected tocpis in Cybersecurity (cryptography, network security, binary analysis e web security
    • Capture-the-Flag (CTF) competition, a jeopardy-style CTF aimed at selecting 8 finalists who will access the next phase.
  2. Advanced Cybersecurity Training
    • 3 hands-on training sessions on advanced Cybersecurity techniques and tools
    • Cyber Defence Simulation, where finalists will be asked to defend a virtual ICT infrastructure from attacks launched by a Red Team

The winners of the Advanced Cybersecurity Training will be awarded 4 scholarships.
